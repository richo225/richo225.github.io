Experience

Babylon Health

Ruby developer• January 2017 - August 2019

Fantastic startup that combines the ever growing computing power of machines and doctors to create a comprehensive health service. I have worked on a variety of projects ranging from front-end portals to designing and building external API's. I spent three months moving all the payments infrastructure from the monolith to ruby microservices for PCI compliance. I was involded in building a stand alone black-box testing suite as well as new payment gateway adapter for global rollout to Saudi Arabia, USA, Canada and other regions.

The last year I was one of two engineers responsible for the initial roll out of the Babylon ecosystem to Canada - building new custom APIs such as those for patient/pharmacy geolocation, medical identifier processing and insurance redemption for different provinces. The project involved frequent deployment of the monolith and core microservices using kubernetes and jenkins to the production environment as well as migrating existing identity document data from UK Onfido servers to our cloud based AWS infrastructure in Canada using ActiveStorage.
